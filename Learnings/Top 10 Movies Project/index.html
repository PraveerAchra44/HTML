My top ten movies

Harry Potter

In a magical world filled with witches and wizards, a young orphan named Harry Potter discovers he is a wizard on his 11th birthday. He is sent to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. Together, they uncover a hidden prophecy that predicts the return of the dark wizard Lord Voldemort. Harry learns about his tragic past and his connection to Voldemort. As the trio battles dark creatures and encounters challenges, Harry's bravery and loyalty are tested. With the support of his friends, Harry confronts Voldemort in an epic showdown, ultimately triumphing over evil and saving the wizarding world.

Clouds

"Clouds" is a heartfelt film based on a true story. It follows the inspiring journey of Zach Sobiech, a talented teenager diagnosed with a rare form of bone cancer. Determined to leave a lasting impact on the world, Zach pours his emotions into music and becomes a viral sensation with his song, "Clouds." Amidst the struggles of his illness and the challenges of adolescence, Zach embraces love, friendship, and his passion for music. The movie explores themes of hope, resilience, and the power of living life to the fullest, as Zach's legacy continues to touch the hearts of millions.
